About

Profile

I'm a final-year Aeronautical Engineering student at KLS Gogte Institute of Technology, Belagavi, with a broad foundation in mechanical design, simulation, UAV development, robotics, and advanced manufacturing.

My toolkit spans ANSYS FEA (static, transient, modal, explicit dynamics, CFD), CAD in SolidWorks, CATIA, Fusion 360 and AutoCAD, programming in Python and MATLAB, and hands-on additive manufacturing (FDM/SLA) with strong DFM/DFA principles. I'm equally comfortable designing for flight, building robotic systems, or running simulation-driven design validation.

I've competed in and won ASME EFx India four consecutive times (SDC & IAM3D, 2025–2026), delivering real engineered hardware under tight competition constraints across UAV, robotics, and precision mechanism domains.
